Intelligence Data Sources
All sources public · Free or open access · No scraping of private data
DRI Press Releases
Enforcement Intelligence
Directorate of Revenue Intelligence publishes individual seizure press releases in real-time at dri.nic.in. Structured data source for seizure weight, location, and method.
Public — dri.nic.in
World Gold Council
Market Intelligence
WGC publishes quarterly gold demand trends, central bank data, mine production, ETF flows, and annual supply data. Downloadable datasets at gold.org/goldhub/data
Free download — gold.org
India TradeStats
Trade Flow Data
Ministry of Commerce trade statistics portal. HS Code 7108 (gold) import/export data available 2017–present. Monthly granularity by country of origin.
Public — tradestat.commerce.gov.in
Freegoldapi.com
Price Data API
768 years of historical gold price data. CORS-enabled, no API key. Daily updates via GitHub Actions. XAU/USD from 1258 to present. Silver ratios included.
Free API · No key needed
metals.dev
Live Price API
Real-time precious metals prices including MCX India gold price, LBMA London fix, and FOREX rates. Free tier available with rate limits.
Free API · metals.dev
Rajya Sabha Q&A
Parliamentary Record
Finance Ministry submits gold seizure statistics to Parliament in written replies. All public record. Primary source for airport-wise seizure breakdowns cited in media.
Public — sansad.in
data.gov.in
Government Open Data
DGFT and MoCI datasets on India's gold keyword resources. Open Government Data Platform — multiple gold trade datasets with API access.
Open API — data.gov.in
DMCC Trade Data
UAE Gold Trade
Dubai Multi Commodities Centre publishes annual gold trade reports. UAE is India's largest gold supplier. DMCC member refinery list is public.
Public — dmcc.ae
IMF GFSR / Article IV
Monetary Intelligence
IMF publishes gold reserve data for member countries, AML/CFT assessments for precious metals sectors, and geopolitical risk analysis affecting gold markets.
Free — imf.org
About Bucks Intelligence

Bucks Intelligence is the supply chain data layer for the Bucks open protocol. It aggregates publicly available gold flow data — enforcement records, trade statistics, price feeds, and regulatory changes — into structured intelligence for compliance teams, bullion dealers, banks, and researchers.


All data on this platform is sourced from public government records, official international organisations, and free market data APIs. No private, proprietary, or confidential data is used. This is not financial advice.
